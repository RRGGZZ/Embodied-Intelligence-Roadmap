# 3-Year Technical Evolution Path

## Year 1: Foundation & Control Backbone

Goals: - Deep understanding of RL algorithms - Master MPC and NMPC
frameworks - Build simulation proficiency (Isaac Lab) - Implement
baseline humanoid locomotion

Milestones: - Reproduce RL-MPC hybrid paper - Implement simple
whole-body control - Deploy sim2real on small-scale platform

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Year 2: Hybrid Intelligence & Robustness

Goals: - Develop stable RL + MPC integration - Improve contact-aware
locomotion - Study motion priors and retargeting - Build deployable
humanoid policy stack

Milestones: - Publish RL-MPC or sim2real-related work - Achieve stable
multi-contact control - Real hardware experiments

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Year 3: Generalized Humanoid Intelligence

Goals: - Integrate foundation models - Develop scalable
locomotion-manipulation policies - Improve cross-platform transfer -
Design unified control architecture

Milestones: - Submit high-impact robotics paper - Demonstrate robust
loco-manipulation on hardware - Build reproducible research framework

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Evolution Flow:

Motion Prior ↓ RL Policy ↓ RL-MPC Hybrid ↓ Whole-Body Control ↓ Sim2Real
↓ Hardware Deployment

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This roadmap will be refined every 6 months.
